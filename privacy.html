Overview

AI Agent Dock ("we", "us", or "the site") is a static directory website that helps developers find and copy AI coding assets — rules, roles, skills, hooks, workflows and MCP configs for Cursor, Windsurf, Claude Code, GitHub Copilot, Codex and MCP-compatible tools. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little personal information as possible.

Information We Do Not Collect

This site does not require account registration, login, or any form submission to browse or copy assets. We do not actively collect sensitive personal information such as:

  • Full name, home address, or government ID numbers
  • Payment or financial information
  • Health, biometric, or other special-category data
  • Passwords or authentication credentials

If you contact us by email, we will only use the information you provide to respond to your message.

You can manage ad personalization through your browser settings or industry opt-out tools provided by ad networks.

Cookies & Local Storage

The core site functionality (search, filters, copy) runs entirely in your browser and does not require cookies. Third-party analytics and advertising scripts may set cookies as described above.

Changes & Contact
